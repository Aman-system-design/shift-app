# SHIFT ◆ Command Center

**Military-grade accountability for your personal life.**

SHIFT turns your life goals into work shifts — because you never miss a shift at work. Why should your personal goals be any different?

## Why SHIFT?

You don't procrastinate at work. You show up for 6:30 AM shifts. You meet deadlines. You perform.

But personal goals? CAT prep? Fitness? You "do it later." The difference isn't discipline — it's **structure**.

SHIFT recreates the exact conditions that make you perform at work:
- **Fixed shifts** — not "I'll study sometime"
- **Clock in/out** — like punching in at work  
- **AWOL detection** — miss a shift and it's on record
- **Performance reviews** — weekly truth reports
- **Real-time accountability** — notifications, check-ins, timers

## Features

🕐 **Command Center** — Real-time clock, waking hours draining, CAT exam countdown, today's schedule  
⏱ **Clock In/Out** — Timer, 30-min check-ins ("Still on task, soldier?"), debrief on clock-out  
📊 **Intel Report** — Hours by category, completion rate, streaks, brutally honest truth section  
⚠ **AWOL Alert** — Full-screen red alert when you miss a shift  
💀 **Stoic Quotes** — Rotating Seneca/Marcus Aurelius wisdom about time  
📱 **PWA** — Installable, works offline, push notifications  

## Tech

Pure HTML + CSS + JavaScript. No frameworks. No dependencies. Just discipline.
